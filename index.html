About KDMedia

We've been
in the room

KDMedia was founded by Kristoffer Douglas — an Emmy Award-winning media professional with 15+ years of experience inside some of the most demanding broadcast, digital, and brand environments in the industry.

Our team has produced content for the 2008 Beijing Olympics at NBCUniversal, built award-winning campaigns at NESN for the Red Sox and Bruins, scaled FantasyPros from a thousand followers to a multimedia powerhouse, and led creative across global brands at Rodale Inc.

We bring that experience to every client — regardless of size. Because we believe great creative work isn't reserved for the biggest brands. It's for the most ambitious ones.
