About KDMedia

More than
media —
a mission.

KDMedia is the creative studio of Kristoffer Douglas — an Emmy Award-winning media professional with over 15 years of experience and a career spanning broadcast television, digital media, Hollywood development, and brand strategy.

From producing content for the 2008 Beijing Olympics at NBCUniversal, to leading creative at NESN for the Red Sox and Bruins, to scaling FantasyPros from 1,100 to 200,000+ YouTube subscribers — Kris brings a rare blend of editorial discipline, creative vision, and measurable results to every engagement.

With a deep commitment to non-profits and community initiatives, KDMedia isn't just an agency — it's a strategic partner for organizations ready to communicate with clarity, creativity, and conviction.
